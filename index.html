📜 Restrictive Covenants & Community Rules

The official Spruce Meadows Subdivision No. 4 Restrictive Covenants govern all properties in this neighborhood. Key highlights include:

  • All building plans require written Architectural Committee approval before construction.
  • Minimum home size: 1,300 sq ft (single-story); 1,400 sq ft (two-story/tri-level); 2,100 sq ft (with basement).
  • All homes must include brick or stone on the front and a minimum 4-in-12 roof pitch.
  • Enclosed garage required (2–3 car capacity).
  • Fences must not extend closer than 20 ft to street, and no higher than 6 ft without approval.
  • No more than 2 dogs/cats per household. Farm animals prohibited.
  • No boats, trailers, RVs, or unsightly vehicles parked outside enclosed garages.
  • Front yard landscaping to be completed within 30 days of occupancy.
  • Weeds must be cut to under 4 inches.
  • Satellite dishes/antennas outside buildings require Architectural Committee approval.
